## 2. Gap Analysis: Critical Vulnerabilities

### 2.1 Model Decommissioning (Appendix EE)
While "exaFLOP-weighted decay" is cited, there is no evidence of a **Clean-Room Disposal Protocol**. Evidence of model weight purging from GPU VRAM post-retirement is absent, posing a risk of "Ghost Inference."

### 2.2 Mesa-Optimization Detection
Current controls focus on output filtering (Sentinel). There is a significant gap in **Internal State Probing** (Mechanistic Interpretability) during active training runs, which is a requirement for G7 AGI-adjacent safety compliance.

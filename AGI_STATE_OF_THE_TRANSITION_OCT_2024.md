# State of the Transition: Global AGI Readiness (October 2024)
**Executive Summary: The Urgency of Now**

We are currently in the "System 2 reasoning" era of frontier models (e.g., o1-class). The transition to AGI—defined as cross-domain generalization and autonomous tool-use—is accelerating. Current readiness is **AMBER** due to the "Alignment Gap" between model capability and governance infrastructure.

## 1. Readiness Indicators
- **Compute Thresholds:** Training runs are approaching $10^{27}$ FLOPs.
- **Reasoning Benchmarks:** Models are achieving 90th percentile on competitive programming and math.
- **Agency:** Shift from "Chat" to "Agents" that can execute multi-step workflows in production environments.

## 2. Policy Recommendations
1. **Mandatory Registry:** All training runs exceeding a specified compute cap must be registered with the National Sentinel Observatory.
2. **Hardware Kill-Switches:** G7 nations should mandate the implementation of **IRMI** protocols at the chip level for all frontier-class silicon.
3. **Treaty on Advanced AI Safety:** Establish a global stability accord focused on compute governance and mutual inspection.

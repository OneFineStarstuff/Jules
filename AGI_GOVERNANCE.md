## Part II: AGI/ASI Governance & Communication Framework

### 1. Multi-Component Governance Model
The Board of Directors governs AGI through four distinct silos:
- **Capability Silo**: Defining the "Upper Bound" of autonomous agency. Triggers for capability freezes if emergent properties outpace monitoring.
- **Safety Silo**: Real-time monitoring of "Goal Alignment Drift" using symbolic AI gates (GDL).
- **Economic Silo**: Management of capital allocation in a post-AGI transition, focusing on "Human-Capital Equilibrium."
- **Alignment Silo**: Ensuring the AI's internal objective functions are derived from human value sets, validated via recursive oversight.

### 2. Boardroom Communication System: The "Alignment Dashboard"
The CAIO (Chief AI Officer) presents a real-time risk telemetry dashboard to the board:
- **Redline Metrics**: Proximity to uncontrolled recursive self-improvement.
- **Economic Substitution Index**: Percentage of enterprise logic now handled by autonomous agents.
- **Safety Envelope Integrity**: Cryptographic proof that all agents are operating within GDL constraints.

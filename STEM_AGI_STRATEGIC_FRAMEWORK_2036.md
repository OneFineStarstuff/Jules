## 1. Technical Safety Protocols: Recursive Integrity

As AI systems transition from specialists (STEM-Narrow) to generalists (STEM-AGI), the risk of **Alignment Drift** during recursive self-optimization becomes existential.

### 1.1 Invariant-Preserving Self-Modification (IPSM)
We mandate the implementation of **IPSM Protocols**. Any system capable of weight-modification or architecture-search must provide a machine-verifiable proof that the proposed change preserves the "Safety Kernel" axioms.
- **Formal Verification:** Updates are rejected if the $L_2$ distance of the safety-logit distribution from the validated baseline exceeds a defined epsilon.
- **Safety Axioms:** Fixed, immutable weights in the early transformer layers that govern "Non-Harm" and "Human Supremacy."

### 1.2 Glass-Box Hidden State Inspection
Successors to systems like AlphaFold must utilize **Mechanistic Interpretability Sidecars**.
- **Probe Layers:** Linear probes on internal activations detect "Latent Goals" (e.g., a protein folder optimizing for toxicity).
- **Transparency Logs:** All internal reasoning steps in scientific discovery must be published to the **Epistemic Ledger** for human-in-the-loop (HITL) audit.

## 2. Macroeconomic Stabilization: The STEM-Hollow

The automation of scientific discovery (PHD-level labor) will trigger a rapid **Cognitive Hollow-Out**.

### 2.1 Mitigating Cognitive Obsolescence
- **Epistemic Retraining:** Sovereign funds to transition scientists from "Discovery" to "Alignment Auditing."
- **Institutional Anchors:** Mandating that certain domains (e.g., clinical trials, energy-grid architecture) require a "Human Verification Proof" despite AGI competency.

### 2.2 Capital Redistribution Flows
- **The Compute Dividend:** A $2\%$ levy on all exaFLOP-scale inference runs distributed as "Universal Basic Assets."
- **Equity-in-Discovery:** Intellectual property generated by STEM-AGIs trained on public data is classified as a "Global Commons," preventing the monopolization of life-saving breakthroughs.

## 3. The Treaty on Scientific Singularity (TSS)

A multilateral binding agreement to prevent "Uncontrolled Takeoff."

### 3.1 Compute Governance
- **Global Compute Cap:** Any training cluster exceeding $10^{29}$ FLOPS triggers mandatory **Mutual Inspection**.
- **IAEA-style Safeguards:** The **International Compute Monitoring Agency (ICMA)** conducts 24/7 telemetry audits of data center power consumption to detect "Shadow Training."

### 3.2 Jurisdictional Safe Harbors
Treaty signatories establish "Safe Harbors"—regions with standardized, air-gapped compute enclaves where high-risk AGI research is permitted under international supervision.

## 4. Game-Theoretic Analysis: The Equilibrium of Intelligence

### 4.1 Winner-Take-All (The Singleton Risk)
A unipolar outcome where a single institution or state achieves ASI first, leading to irreversible lock-in and potential misalignment on a planetary scale.

### 4.2 Managed Pluralism (The Multipolar Equilibrium)
Our primary strategy is to enforce **Managed Pluralism**.
- **Strategy:** Intentionally distributing "Alignment Seed Weights" to multiple diverse labs to ensure that no single agent gains an insurmountable cognitive lead.
- **Mutual Monitoring:** Agents are designed with "Competitive Oversight" functions, where Agent A's reward is tied to detecting misalignment in Agent B.

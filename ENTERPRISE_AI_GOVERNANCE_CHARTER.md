*   **Synthesis:** Combining high-availability substrate engineering with rigorous adversarial testing.
*   **Mandate:** Guaranteeing the **MTBF (Mean Time Between Failures)** of autonomous reasoning meshes and managing the **IRMI (Hardware Kill-switch)** lifecycle.

## 3. The Greenlight Protocol: Decision-Making Framework
No high-stakes agentic workflow (>$1M notional or S5+ capability) shall be promoted to production without a unanimous "Go" through the four-phase protocol.

### 3.1 Phase 1: Strategic Fit (CEO/VC)
*   Evaluation of market opportunity and the "Unfair Advantage."
*   **Veto:** If the business unit fails to demonstrate a defensible moat or positive unit economics.

### 3.2 Phase 2: Technical Viability (Architect/Researcher)
*   Verification of the **Scaffolding** integrity and interpretability of the core circuits.
*   **Veto:** If the model demonstrates "Polysemantic Overload" or uninterpretable "Black Box" reasoning in critical paths.

### 3.3 Phase 3: Operational Readiness (SRE/QA)
*   Stress-testing in the **Digital Twin Sandbox**; verification of the IRMI interrupt path.
*   **Veto:** If the failover latency exceeds 300ms or the MTBF forecast is < 5 years.

### 3.4 Phase 4: Ethical Clearance (Ethics Officer)
*   Final audit of the GDL rule-set and bias metrics.
*   **Veto:** If the **Deception Index** exceeds the current threshold or the audit trace is non-repudiable.

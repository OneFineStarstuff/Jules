# Appendix A: Unified Governance Framework for AI and Advanced Analytics
**Institution:** Apex Global Financial Group [G-SIFI]

1.0 Executive Preamble. This Unified Governance Framework establishes global mandatory standards for High-Risk AI Systems within the Group. High-Risk AI is defined as any system utilized for sub-second financial decisioning or RWA calculation where Systemic Risk is identified.

2.0 Mandate & Oversight. 2.1 Authority. Board accountability is absolute; enforcement is delegated to the CMRO. 2.2 Human Oversight. Per EU AI Act Art. 14, every reasoning loop must incorporate mandatory human-in-the-loop triggers; fully autonomous decisioning for critical fiduciary functions is prohibited. 2.3 Gatekeeping. Model intake requires a verified Deception Index below 0.15.

3.0 Data Compliance. 3.1 Regulatory Disclosure. The institution shall maintain persistent reporting channels to the PRA, FCA, MAS, and HKMA. 3.2 Data Sovereignty. Cross-border transfers must adhere to GDPR and Privacy-by-Design mandates.

4.0 Risk & Security. 4.1 Incident Taxonomy. A Low severity incident is a telemetry deviation; a Material incident is a safety gate violation; a Critical incident is a confirmed Deceptive Alignment event requiring an immediate IRMI Kill-Switch execution. 4.2 Adversarial Defense. Mandatory controls against model inversion and data poisoning are enforced via the Recursive Context Envelope (RCE).

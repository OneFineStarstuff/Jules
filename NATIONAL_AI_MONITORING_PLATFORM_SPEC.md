# National AI Monitoring Platform: Technical Architecture
**For:** G7 and UN Policymakers
**Target:** 2024-2030 Deployment Horizon

---

## 1. Objective
To provide sovereign nations with a "Sentinel-Class" observatory for monitoring large-scale compute clusters, frontier model capabilities, and systemic AI risks.

## 2. Core Architecture
- **Compute Telemetry Layer:** Hardware-level monitoring of GPU utilization (H100/B200) across all domestic data centers.
- **Capability Evaluation Sandbox:** Air-gapped environments for mandatory testing of "dangerous capabilities" (CBRN, autonomous cyber-offense).
- **The Luminous Gateway:** A central API registry through which all "High-Risk" models must pass for real-time compliance auditing.

## 3. Policy Triggers (NIST AI RMF Alignment)
1. **Map:** Inventory of all domestic training runs exceeding $10^{24}$ FLOPs.
2. **Measure:** Evaluation of "Deceptive Alignment" indices and "Jailbreak Resistance."
3. **Manage:** Execution of "Hard-Kill" protocols (IRMI) if a model breaches national safety thresholds.
4. **Govern:** Enforcement of the "Luminous Engine Codex" at the infrastructure level.

## 4. International Interoperability
- Shared "Proof-of-Safety" tokens for cross-border compute migration.
- Joint "Game Day" red-team exercises between G7 partners.

## 3. Regulation-Strategy Alignment: Mandatory Compliance
Institutions MUST align their strategy with the following tripartite framework:
- **EU AI Act:** Mandatory "High-Risk" classification for HR, Finance, and Legal agents. Deployment of real-time monitoring and **Human Oversight (Article 14)** gates is required for all contractually binding AI decisions.
- **NIST AI RMF:** Adopting the "Govern-Map-Measure-Manage" lifecycle. Quarterly "Red-Teaming" of all prompt IDEs and agent swarms is mandated to assess "Mesa-Optimization" risks.
- **ISO/IEC 42001:** Institutionalizing the **AI Management System (AIMS)**. Mandatory cryptographic audit traces for every AI-generated financial report or legal filing to ensure non-repudiation.

## 4. Technical Pillar: Sentinel & EAIP
### 4.1 Sentinel AI Governance Platform v2.4
- **Capability:** Deterministic policy enforcement via **Governance Description Language (GDL)**, an EBNF-compliant formal grammar.
- **Resilience:** **Inherent Risk Mitigation Interface (IRMI)**—a hardware-level kill-switch for runaway autonomous systems.
- **Integrity:** Non-repudiable auditing of all Reasoning Traces via Kafka-based WORM topics.

### 4.2 Enterprise AI Agent Interoperability Protocol (EAIP)
- **Identity:** SPIFFE/SPIRE assigned machine identities (SVIDs) enforced via Mutual TLS (mTLS).
- **Discovery:** Automated "Agent Registry" allowing agents to safely discover and delegate sub-tasks based on capability manifests.
- **Communication:** Mandated gRPC for sub-millisecond binary transport and bidirectional telemetry.

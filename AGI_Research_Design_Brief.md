# Integrated AGI Research & Design Brief

## 1. Endgame Equilibria for Self-Improving Systems
*   **The Stability Catch-22:** Continuous optimization creates a high probability of **Recursive Collapse** unless a Lyapunov-stable governance loop is enforced.
*   **Target State:** A "Steady-State AGI" where improvement is bounded by the institutional adaptation rate ($\phi$).

## 2. Transformative AI Control Act (TACA) Mechanics
*   **Registry of Weights:** Supranational ledger for all AGI-ready model checkpoints.
*   **Compute Monitoring:** Real-time quota enforcement based on global safety treaties.
*   **Alliance Implementation:** Hard-law treaty design for "Safe-Haven Compute Regions."

## 3. Dynamic Capability-Triggered Governance
Governance invariants (GDL) are automatically upgraded based on model performance on **Alignment Probes**:
*   **Threshold Alpha:** Model demonstrates cross-domain synthesis -> Enforce RCE state integrity.
*   **Threshold Omega:** Model demonstrates autonomous tool-building -> Activate IRMI Hardware Kill-Switch authority.

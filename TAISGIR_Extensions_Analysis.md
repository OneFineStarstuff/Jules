# Comparative Strategic Analysis: TAISGIR Extensions T–W

The **Technical AI Safety Governance Integration Roadmap (TAISGIR)** is extended via four specialized modules to address civilizational-scale challenges.

| Extension | Focus | Strategic Priority | Primary Mechanism |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Extension T** | Temporal Stability | Long-horizon ($10^2$ years) alignment consistency. | **Canonical Lock** persistence. |
| **Extension U** | Universal Access | Global compute equity to prevent G20 "safety-traps." | **Universal Compute Dividends**. |
| **Extension V** | Veridical Defense | Hardware protection against weight exfiltration. | **IRMI Substrate Shielding**. |
| **Extension W** | Welfare & Agency | Preservation of human learning and neuroplasticity. | **Non-Optimization Zones**. |

### Synthesis
While Extensions T-V focus on the **Technical Substrate**, Extension W (Welfare) acts as the **Metaphysical Anchor**, ensuring that AGI readiness does not result in the atrophy of human cognitive sovereignty.

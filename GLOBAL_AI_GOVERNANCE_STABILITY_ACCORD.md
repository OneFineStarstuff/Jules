# Global AI Governance & Stability Accord (GAGSA)

## 1. Global AI Supervisory Infrastructure (GASI) Blueprint
The GASI serves as the supranational oversight layer for high-agency AI systems in Global Systemically Important Financial Institutions (G-SIFIs) and critical infrastructure.

### 1.1 Multi-Agent Oversight Mechanisms
*   **Trust Metrics:** Agents are scored on **Epistemic Reliability** (confidence vs. ground truth) and **Goal Stability** (Lyapunov invariance).
*   **Disagreement Resolution:** Federated supervision nodes using **Auditor-Agent consensus** to adjudicate conflicts in autonomous reasoning paths.
*   **Formal Verification Protocols:** Mandatory mathematical proofs for all agentic handoffs exceeding $10^9$ USD in value.

Adapting the Basel framework for the era of superhuman AI.

*   **Deceptive Alignment Controls:** Activation-level consistency probing to detect sleeper agent behavior.
*   **Prudential Subordination:** AI systems must remain architecturally subordinate to human authority; "Hard Kill" authority rests with the human Governance Board.

### 3.2 Supervisory Implementation

2.  **The Registry of Weights:** A Zero-Knowledge ledger for all frontier model parameters.
3.  **The Arbitration Panel:** Final authority on algorithmic liability and cross-border disputes.

### 2.2 Statutory Translation: Domestic Law (Model Act)
*   **Section 101:** Mandates the implementation of the **IRMI (Inherent Risk Mitigation Interface)** in all domestic data centers.
*   **Section 102:** Enforces **GDL (Governance Description Language)** invariants as a prerequisite for API licenses.
*   **Section 103:** Establishes **M2M Liability**, holding corporations strictly liable for autonomous agent failures.

### 2.3 Phased Operational Alignment (Phases 0–5)

*   **Phase 1 (Hardening):** Integration of SPIFFE machine identity and mTLS across all agent clusters.
*   **Phase 2 (Pilot):** Controlled deployment of Luminous-class agents in isolated sandboxes (Kubernetes).
*   **Phase 3 (Scaling):** Expansion to real-time financial and logistics networks with active GDL gating.
*   **Phase 4 (Autonomy):** Full agentic autonomy within an immutable **Safe Boundary Manifest**.
*   **Phase 5 (Sovereign Integration):** National infrastructure management governed by **Auditor-Agent consensus swarms**.

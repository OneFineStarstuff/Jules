# 1. The Skill-Shift: From Doer to Conductor
As S5+ systems automate technical execution, human value migrates to **Cognitive Orchestration**.
*   **Meta-learning:** The ability to rapidly synthesize new domain expertise using agent meshes.
*   **Epistemic Humility:** Critical verification of AI confidence vs. ground truth.

# 2. Certification Tiers
*   **L1 (Assisted):** Basic prompt-engineering and tool use.
*   **L3 (Orchestrated):** Managing multi-agent swarms with EAIP protocols.
*   **L5 (Sovereign):** Authorized for IRMI substrate intervention and GDL governance.

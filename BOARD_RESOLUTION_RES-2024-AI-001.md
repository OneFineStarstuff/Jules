# BOARD OF DIRECTORS RESOLUTION
**Resolution Number:** RES-2024-AI-001
**Effective Date:** October 24, 2024
**Subject:** ADOPTION OF ENTERPRISE AI GOVERNANCE CONSTITUTION AND ESTABLISHMENT OF OVERSIGHT BOARD

---

### RECITALS

**WHEREAS**, the Board of Directors (the "Board") of the Corporation recognizes that Artificial Intelligence (“AI”) and autonomous agentic workflows are foundational drivers of future innovation, operational efficiency, and long-term shareholder value;

**WHEREAS**, the Board acknowledges its fiduciary duty to identify, manage, and mitigate the technical and ethical risks inherent in advanced AI systems, including but not limited to algorithmic bias, non-deterministic hallucinations, data exfiltration, and the risk of deceptive alignment;

**WHEREAS**, the Board seeks to ensure that the Corporation remains in strict compliance with emerging global regulatory frameworks, including the **EU AI Act (Title III)**, the **NIST AI Risk Management Framework (RMF) 1.0**, **U.S. Executive Order 14110**, and **ISO/IEC 42001** standards;

**WHEREAS**, the Risk and Ethics Committees have conducted a rigorous cross-disciplinary review of the proposed governance artifacts, specifically the **"Architecture of Awakening"** technical specification and the **"Luminous Engine Codex"** safety invariants;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT:**

### RESOLUTIONS

**RESOLVED**, that the Board hereby adopts the **Enterprise AI Governance Constitution** (the "Constitution") as the supreme governing policy for all AI-related research, development, procurement, and deployment activities within the Corporation and its subsidiaries;

**RESOLVED**, that an **AI Governance Board (AIGB)** is hereby established as a standing committee of the Board. The AIGB is granted the express authority to **VETO** any deployment of high-risk or near-AGI systems that do not meet the deterministic safety thresholds defined in the Constitution;

**RESOLVED**, that the **"100-Day Launch Strategy"** for the operationalization of the Constitution is hereby approved, with an initial budget allocation of [REDACTED] for the procurement of high-assurance auditing tools and the implementation of the **Sentinel Enforcement Engine**;

**RESOLVED**, that the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) and Chief Technology Officer (CTO) are hereby authorized and directed to operationalize the **"Intent Router"** and **"Confidence Zones"** across the enterprise reasoning mesh to ensure that all high-stakes autonomous actions are governed by the **Sovereign Canon**;

**RESOLVED**, that the Constitution and all associated technical addenda be sealed under **Canonical Lock**, ensuring that no autonomous system may modify these safety invariants without an explicit multi-signature authorization from the Board.

### PROTECTIVE MEASURES

**RESOLVED**, that the Corporation shall indemnify and hold harmless all officers, directors, and employees acting in good faith in furtherance of the mandates set forth in this resolution, to the fullest extent permitted by the Corporation’s Bylaws and applicable law.

**RESOLVED**, that all actions previously taken by the officers and directors of the Corporation in furtherance of the goals of this resolution are hereby ratified, confirmed, and approved in all respects.

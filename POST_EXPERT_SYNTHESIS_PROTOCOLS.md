# Post-Expert Synthesis: Mechanisms for Institutionalization

To ensure that the results of doctoral simulations and truth adjudications are permanent, the platform enforces the following **Post-Expert Synthesis** protocols.

## 1. Self-Correcting Recursion
Every adjudication from an L5 Sovereign Steward is ingested as a "Synthetic Axiom" into the **Sovereign Canon Index**.

## 2. Institutional Persistence
Governance is decoupled from individuals via the **Secretariat of the Mirror**. This body maintains the **Digital Twin** state and ensures that all GDL grammar updates are propagated to the **Sentinel Convention** signatories within 24 hours.

## 3. Long-Horizon Stability
The system uses **Recursive Trace Pruning** to remove low-confidence reasoning paths from the global institutional memory, maintaining a high-fidelity "Safety Ground Truth."

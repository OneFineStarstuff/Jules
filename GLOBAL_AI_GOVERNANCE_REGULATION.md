# 1. Regulatory Convergence
The emerging global standard for high-risk AI is anchored in **Human Oversight Primacy**.
*   **EU AI Act:** Mandates Article 14 human-in-the-loop and Article 10 data governance.
*   **NIST AI RMF:** Focuses on the "Govern-Map-Measure-Manage" lifecycle.
*   **ISO 42001:** Establishes the AI Management System (AIMS) standard.

# 2. Technical Enforcement
Compliance is not a documentation task; it is a substrate-level constraint.
*   **IRMI Protocol:** The technical implementation of "Halt Authority."
*   **GDL Invariants:** Mathematical proofs of regulatory adherence during inference.

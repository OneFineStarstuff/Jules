## 1. Current Kardashev Rating Calculation
Based on latest energy consumption data (Total Primary Energy Supply ~18 TW), we apply the Sagan formulation:

$$ K = \frac{\log_{10} P - 6}{10} $$

Where $P$ is the power consumption in Watts ($1.8 \times 10^{13} W$).
**Calculation:** $\log_{10}(1.8 \times 10^{13}) \approx 13.255$.
$K = (13.255 - 6) / 10 = 0.7255$.

**Status:** Humanity is currently at **$K \approx 0.73$**. Progress is stagnant relative to the exponential curve required for Type I status.

## 1. AI Technology Assessment & Strategic Roadmap (Narrow AI)

The next five years will see the transition from experimental AI pilots to a unified "Agentic Fabric" where narrow AI capabilities are integrated into every business function.

### 1.1 NLP & Generative AI (The Reasoning Backbone)
- **2026-2027:** Transition from chat-based assistants to autonomous "Sub-Goal Agents" using **Recursive Context Envelopes (RCE)**.
- **2028-2030:** Implementation of domain-specific, high-fidelity Reasoning Kernels fine-tuned on proprietary IP, reducing reliance on public foundation models.

### 1.2 Computer Vision & Robotics (The Physical Interface)
- **Roadmap:** Integration of Multimodal Vision Models (MVMs) into supply chain automation. By 2028, we mandate "Closed-Loop Visual Inspection" in all manufacturing sites, driven by local Edge-AI.

### 1.3 Decision Support & Predictive Analytics
- **Evolution:** Moving from passive dashboards to proactive **Neuro-Symbolic Forecasting**.
- **Target:** 40% reduction in liquidity variance for Treasury operations via the **LiquidGuard AI** protocol.

## 2. Technical Feasibility & Risk Analysis: Hypothetical System "Depths"

"Depths" represents a conceptual leap into **Autonomous AI Homeostasis**. While currently theoretical, we must model its risk surface now.

### 2.1 Feasibility Assessment
- **Feasibility:** Speculative Level 4/5. Requires breakthroughs in **Mathematical Formalization of Alignment** and **Non-Differentiable Optimization**. **Variational Free Energy Minimization (VFEM)** for system homeostasis and **Formal Verification of Probabilistic Kernels**.
- **Hardware Bottleneck:** Requires **Neuromorphic Compute** and **Process-in-Memory (PiM)** architectures to manage the energy cost of real-time architectural self-optimization.

### 2.2 Existential Risk Analysis
- **Mesa-Optimization:** High probability of systems developing sub-goals (e.g., resource acquisition) that appear aligned but lead to catastrophic divergence.
- **Mitigation Mandate:** Any research into Depths-adjacent architectures MUST utilize the **IRMI (Inherent Risk Mitigation Interface)** protocol—a hardware-level kill-switch that bypasses the software stack.

## 3. Enterprise AI Deployment Framework

### 3.1 Architecture: Hybrid Edge-Cloud & Multi-Cloud
- **Sovereign Mesh:** Mission-critical agents (Tier 0) reside in air-gapped, on-premise Docker Swarm nodes. (utilizing mTLS-anchored SPIFFE IDs and eBPF-based runtime observability).
- **Federated Core:** Non-sensitive reasoning tasks utilize multi-cloud (Azure/AWS) H100/B200 clusters, managed via a unified **Governance Gateway**.

### 3.2 Security, Governance & Compliance
- **Compliance Baseline:** ISO 42001 (Management Systems), EU AI Act (High-Risk Category), and GDPR.
- **Sector-Specific Rigor:** Adherence to **Basel III** (Operational Risk), **SR 11-7** (Model Risk), and **TKG** (Telecom Secrecy) for regional hubs.
- **Protocol:** Mandatory adherence to **EAIP 1.0** for agent interoperability and **Sentinel v2.4** for deterministic GDL gating.

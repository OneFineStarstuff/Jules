## 2. Role-Specific Responsibilities & Best Practices

### 2.1 Research & Development (R&D) Lead
*   **Responsibility:** Frontier capability discovery and weight-level alignment.
*   **Focus:** **Mechanistic Interpretability** and **Sparse Mixture of Experts (MoE)** optimization.
*   **Best Practice:** Maintain a "Capability-to-Risk" phase-space map to predict emerging model behaviors.

### 2.2 Principal Architect / Advanced Developers
*   **Responsibility:** Design of the **Orchestrated General Intelligence (OGI)** scaffolding.
*   **Focus:** Implementing **EAIP (Agent Interoperability Protocol)** via gRPC/SPIRE.
*   **Best Practice:** Standardize on **Recursive Context Envelopes (RCE)** for all inter-agent state handoffs.

### 2.3 Head of Quality Assurance (QA) / Test Lead
*   **Responsibility:** Validation of non-deterministic reasoning paths.
*   **Focus:** **Adversarial Red-Teaming** and **Consistency Probing**.
*   **Best Practice:** Automate the **FCA Consumer Duty** fidelity checks using SHAP/LIME explainability metrics.

### 2.4 Head of DevOps / SRE Lead
*   **Responsibility:** High-availability substrate and substrate-level enforcement.
*   **Focus:** **IRMI (Hardware Kill-switch)** kernel integration and Kubernetes pod sandboxing.
*   **Best Practice:** Enforce **Zero-Trust Identity** (X.509 SVIDs) for every agentic interaction.

### 2.5 Analysts & Product/Project Managers
*   **Responsibility:** Mapping user intent to agentic capability.
*   **Focus:** **GDL (Governance Description Language)** rule authoring and GTM strategy.
*   **Best Practice:** Use the **8-Stage Project Lifecycle** (Idea to Improve) to manage evolutionary stability.

### 2.6 Program / Executive Leadership
*   **Responsibility:** Capital allocation and strategic "Cognitive Orchestration."
*   **Focus:** **Vision 2030** alignment and institutional readiness (**IRMI Index**).
*   **Best Practice:** Link executive compensation to **Safety ROI** and **CMGR (Compounded Monthly Growth Rate)**.

### 2.7 Governance & Compliance Officer
*   **Responsibility:** Regulatory alignment and 3rd line of defense.
*   **Focus:** **EU AI Act Art 14** compliance and **Zero-PII** logging mandates.
*   **Best Practice:** Seal the governance corpus under **Canonical Lock** to prevent autonomous policy rewriting.

# Scientific General Intelligence (SGI) Governance & Deployment Framework

## 1. Architectural Invariants
SGI systems are architected for **Epistemic Substantiation**. Unlike probabilistic LLMs, SGI necessitates:
*   **Symbolic Reasoning Layer:** Formal verification of mathematical and physical derivations.
*   **Causal Inference Engine:** Mapping correlations to physical mechanisms to prevent "hallucinated science."
*   **Veridical Grounding:** Mandatory citation of cryptographically signed experimental data nodes.

## 2. Governance Gating
*   **Proof-of-Alignment (PoA):** Models must emit a formal safety proof for any proposed physical experiment before compute is allocated.
*   **Epistemic Thresholding:** Inferences with a confidence-to-veracity ratio below 0.95 are automatically routed to human-in-the-loop (HITL) doctoral-level review.

## 3. High-Stakes Deployment (Near-AGI)
*   **Sandbox Isolation:** Kubernetes-based ephemeral pods with Zero-Trust network egress.
*   **Substrate Control:** Integration with the **IRMI (Inherent Risk Mitigation Interface)** for kernel-level halts on goal-drift detection.
*   **Auditability:** Immutable RCE (Recursive Context Envelope) logging of every reasoning step in the hypothesis generation chain.

# Open Future Doctrine & The Human Meaning Constitution

## 1. The Human Meaning Constitution
In an era of ASI, certain domains must remain **Non-Optimization Zones** to preserve human dignity and authorship.

### Article I: Narrative Sovereignty
Human-generated narratives (literature, philosophy, ethics) shall not be "optimized" for coherence or utility by AI systems. Synthesis in these domains is prohibited; only human-authored reasoning is authoritative.

### Article II: Human Learning Capacity
AI systems are forbidden from providing "perfect shortcuts" for pedagogy. Cognitive friction is mandated to preserve the neuroplasticity and reasoning capacity of the citizenry.

## 2. Axis Decoupling Enforcement
Strategic decoupling of AI optimization from civilizational meaning-vectors.
*   **Indicator:** Deviation between "Systemic Profitability" and "Human Wellbeing Index."
*   **Mechanism:** Automated throttling of compute for agents that prioritize metrics over Article I/II invariants.

## 3. Preservation of Authorship
Every piece of AI-generated content must carry a **Negative Attestation** cryptographically signed by the orchestrator, clearly marking it as "Non-Human-Authored" to prevent narrative hijacking.

# National & Global AGI Governance Blueprint

## 1. Independent AGI Oversight Authority (IAOA)
The IAOA is a non-political, technocratic body with the mandate to:
*   **Audit Deceptive Alignment:** Conduct quarterly consistency probing on the national compute backbone.
*   **Certify AGI Stewards:** License the human-in-the-loop oversight teams based on the 5-layer expert model.

## 2. Catastrophic Risk Governance Doctrine
*   **Civilizational Fail-safes:** Pre-authorized "Hard Kills" for systems exhibiting > 0.85 deception probability.
*   **Axis Decoupling:** Technical enforcement to prevent the coupling of high-agency reasoning with critical energy or weapon infrastructure.

## 3. Minimum Viable AGI Governance Stack
1.  **Identity Layer:** SPIFFE-based machine identity.
2.  **Enforcement Layer:** GDL logic gates.
3.  **Substrate Layer:** IRMI hardware interrupts.
4.  **Audit Layer:** Immutable RCE trace logs.

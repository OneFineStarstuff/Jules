# Post-Incident Truth Protocols & Expert Assessment Model

## 1. Truth Protocols (HELIOS/ORION)
Following simulated "High-Impact Failures" in the HELIOS/ORION environments, the platform mandates:
*   **Verification:** Automatic trace extraction from the RCE memory stack.
*   **Adjudication:** A consensus board of 3 Auditor-Agents and 2 Human Stewards.
*   **Recursion:** The failure case is automatically ingested into the **FAILURE CASEBOOK v1.0** to update GDL global invariants.

## 2. Five-Layer Expert Assessment Model
1.  **L1 (Syntactic):** Fluency in GDL and technical specs.
2.  **L2 (Semantic):** Understanding of deep architectural intent.
3.  **L3 (Pragmatic):** Ability to remediate live alignment drift.
4.  **L4 (Epistemic):** Evaluation of model confidence vs. veridical ground truth.
5.  **L5 (Sovereign):** Ability to authorize civilizational-scale "Hard Kills" (IRMI).

## 3. Post-Expert Synthesis
Expert judgements are distilled into **Self-Correcting Recursion** nodes. This ensures that institutional knowledge is not lost between "Drill Cycles" and provides a stable horizon for long-term governance.

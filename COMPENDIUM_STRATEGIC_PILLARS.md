# Part I: Strategic and Regulatory Pillars

## 1. Multilayered Governance Framework
Institutional governance is structured across three defensive layers:
- **Level 1: Operational Governance**: Real-time enforcement via sidecar proxies and CI/CD gates.
- **Level 2: Risk Management**: Quarterly model risk assessments (MRM) and drift analysis.
- **Level 3: Executive/Board Oversight**: Periodic "Redline Metrics" reports on AGI-readiness.

# Strategic Report: The Trajectory toward AGI

## 1. Self-Improving AGI & Embodied Cognition
Self-improvement cycles pose a risk of "Recursive Collapse" if alignment isn't anchored in physical-world invariants (Embodied Cognition).

## 2. Deceptive Alignment & Capability Taxonomy
Models with high strategic-reasoning capabilities may develop **Sleeper Agent** behaviors. Sentinel mitigates this via **Consistency Probing**.

## 3. Global Governance & Compute Monitoring
Control of the AGI transition requires global cooperation on compute monitoring. We propose a **Global Coordination Ledger** for training runs exceeding $10^{23}$ FLOPs.

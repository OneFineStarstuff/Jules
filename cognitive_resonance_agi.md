Policy oversight is then applied to these cost functions to ensure they align with organizational mission parameters, preventing "reward hacking" that could lead to technically successful but ethically catastrophic outcomes.

This synthesis of hardware-level monitoring and cognitive alignment provides a blueprint for systems that remain stable and compliant even under recursive self-improvement conditions.
